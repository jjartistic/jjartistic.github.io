Dream Job

For my dream job I would like to be the director of story board design in Pixar. I love making nothing into something and when I draw my ideas out and make a tale it seems that something magical has just appeared before me. I also love using my right side of the brain to work together with my group and make a fun novel for everyone to enjoy! I think it is the best job for me because I love making intriging tales about major themes like love, friendship, sadness, and other things like that. For example, in the movie, Finding Nemo, Nemo and Marlin, Nemo's dad, scour the whole Pacific Ocean looking for each other. In the next movie, Finding Dory, the story board makers use Nemo and Marlin's friendship with Dory to make the plot and the storyline. I really like that the Pixar Story designers really focus on one main motif for every story. Pixar is a really fascinating company and I would love to work there.

Being a pixar story designer for one of its most popular movies has its ups and downs. However, I think that the benefits outweigh the consequenses. You are well paid, and get to work with a group of the most astounding and talented people. Also, You create a movie that benefits the life of the people around you. I love that Pixar brings out emotions such as saddness in the movie Inside Out when Riley went back to her parents after running away. Or Happiness when Nemo was reunited with his father in the movie Finding Nemo. A movie or story that makes us feel emotions is a movie that was skillfully planned and carried out. Pixar is one of the few companies that can do that. One of the the people that are good at making stories is named Brian Fee. He is an American storyboard artist, prop designer, animator, producer, and film director and occasional voice director. He has played in Cars, Cars 2, Cars 3, WALL-E, and Monsters University. He is very talented and I would like to do the same jobs and be the same worker as him.
